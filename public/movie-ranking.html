Anna's Faves

Top 3 Horror Flicks

Halloween (1978)

The OG! My favorite movie for a cozy night in. Complete with campy dialogue, bad-guy chase scenes, and an annoying little kid who our protagonist must protect at all costs!

The Thing (1982)

An absolute classic! It's got suspense! It's got overly dramatic charactes! It's got a mostly-unseen creature from outer space! What more could you ask for?

Jurassic Park (1993)

Again, a classic! Cheesy dialogue, cheesy characters, cheesy "monsters". All that gooey, cheesy goodness is rolled into a deliciously suspenseful good time.
